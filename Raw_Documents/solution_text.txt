a)  Exemptions  from  Preparing  Consolidated  Financial  Statements  Under  IFRS  10,  a  parent  
may
 
be
 
exempted
 
from
 
preparing
 
consolidated
 
financial
 
statements
 
if

meets
 
all

following
 
four

Condition  Description  
1.  Ownership  Consent  
The  parent  is  a  wholly-owned  subsidiary  or  a  partially-owned  subsidiary,  and  its  other  owners  have  been  informed  about  and  do  not  object  to  the  parent  not  presenting  consolidated  statements.  
2.  Not  Publicly  Traded  
Its  debt  or  equity  instruments  are  not  traded  in  a  public  market  (e.g.,  domestic  or  foreign  stock  exchange).  
3.  No  Regulatory  Filings  
It  did  not  file,  nor  is  it  in  the  process  of  filing,  its  financial  statements  with  a  securities  commission  or  regulatory  body  for  the  purpose  of  issuing  instruments  in  a  public  market.  
4.  Parent  Compliance  
Its  ultimate  or  any  intermediate  parent  produces  consolidated  financial  statements  that  are  available  for  public  use  and  comply  with  IFRSs.
