This Course Book “Investment Management ” has been exclusively written by 
experts in the discipline to up -date your general knowledge of English Language in

Real Rate of Return 
The Percentage Return as calculated above is the nominal rate of return on an 
investment. The nominal return measures how much more money you will have at the 
end of the year if you invest today. It does not tell you the quantu m of goods and 
services you can buy with it. This shortcoming is addressed by the real rate of return. 
The real rate of return tells you how much more you will be able to buy with your return 
on an investment at the end of the year if you invest today. 
   
To convert from nominal to real rate of return, we use the following relationship:  
1 + real rate of return =   1 + nominal rate of return 
   
                                                 1 + Inflation 
Real rate of return =   1 + nominal rate of return   -1 
                                                 1 + Inflation
