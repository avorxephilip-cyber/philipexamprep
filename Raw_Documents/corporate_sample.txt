1.1 Meaning of Corporate Distress or Corporate Sickness 
Distress means acute financial hardship or crisis. Corporate distress or sickness means 
such a situation of a firm when it is unable to meet its debt. In other words, when value 
of the total asset is of a company is insufficient to discharge its total exte rnal liabilities, 
the said company can be said to be a ‘Distress Company’. Corporate Distress also 
includes the following:  
a) unfavourable liquidity position;  
b) adverse long-term solvency position;  
c) outdated production process;  
d) deteriorating selling status;  
e) poor administrative set-up; and  
f) overall adverse economic condition.
